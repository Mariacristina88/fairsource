FairSource

We develop state-of-the art web software for corporate clients. Our software is produced by professionals in the UK and Netherlands, whilst involving people in developing countries. Software produced in this way can carry a Fair Trade label from the Fair Trade Software Foundation, helping you show your commitment to sustainability to your own employees and customers.

We believe that people in developing countries should be given a chance to learn the same skills as the best software professionals in Europe. By involving people in developing countries in our teams, we give them the opportunity to learn. In this way we are helping to grow knowledge economies in developing countries

World Fair Trade Organisation membership is applied for.
